Description

Introductory Narrative: TRACE JOY is more than a sofa. It is the centerpiece of your home, filled with stories, warmth, and playful energy. Designed for those who see their home as a gallery of personal histories, this sofa transforms everyday life into a living myth. Its unique print, inspired by ancient symbols and reimagined in a contemporary style, invites connection and sparks the imagination.

Features as Philosophy:

  • A Playful Landscape: The TRACE JOY modular system allows you to be the curator of your own space. Create the perfect composition for gatherings with friends, cozy evenings, or creative solitude. The modules are easily rearranged, adapting to your rhythm of life.

  • Art as Fabric: The exclusive "JOY" print is a narrative embodied in textile. Bright, warm colors and stylized figures create a visual story that is both dynamic and cozy. The fully removable covers are not only practical but allow you to own a true piece of functional art.

  • Grounded Comfort: The low, relaxed silhouette fosters an informal atmosphere and a sense of stability. The soft, rounded forms and generous proportions are designed for absolute comfort, allowing you to fully immerse yourself in the moment.

The Story of the Hand: The "JOY" print is born from a collaboration with a graphic artist who draws inspiration from petroglyphs and ancient mythology. The idea was to deconstruct archaic images and reassemble them into a joyful, modern pattern. Each symbol on the fabric is a fragment of a larger story that comes to life in your interior, turning furniture into an artifact that connects the past and the present.

Styling Tips from the Founders: From the Architect's Notebook: "The low profile of TRACE JOY is perfect for spaces with high ceilings, creating an interesting contrast. Use it to zone a large room without visually overwhelming it." Designer's Choice: "This print loves warm, natural materials. Pair it with terracotta accents, light oak furniture, and living green plants to create an eclectic and cozy space filled with life. Add a simple wool rug to 'ground' the composition."

FAQ

Are the sofa covers removable, and can I order new ones from upcoming collections?

Yes, all covers are removable, empowering you to refresh your space. You can order new designs from our exclusive collections, updated regularly—stay tuned via our site.

Which countries do you deliver to, and is delivery charged separately?

SĂCRUM delivers to more than 80 countries worldwide. Delivery of all orders is free of charge.

How can I be sure the fabric prints won’t fade or peel?

We use premium European fabrics, certified and tested for 100,000 Martindale cycles. With water-repellent and anti-claw impregnation, prints will stay vibrant for over 10 years with standard use.

How long will delivery take?

Delivery times vary by location: 7-30 days across Europe, 20-60 days worldwide. Exact timelines depend on your location—confirm with our manager at checkout.

Can I return the sofa if it doesn’t suit me?

We do not offer returns as the product is custom-made for you. However, if an unforeseen issue arises, contact our support team—we’ll strive to find a solution, as your satisfaction is our priority.

How should I care for the covers to ensure longevity?

Gently wipe light stains with a damp cloth. For stubborn spots, use textile cleaners, testing them on a hidden area first. Avoid sunlight to preserve vibrancy—your masterpiece deserves to shine.

Are there weight or usage limits for the sofa?

There are no load limits. Crafted for your lifestyle from the most durable materials, the wooden and plywood frame ensures unmatched reliability.

Can I modify the color or configuration?

Yes, our modular design allows adjustments. Write to our manager to explore color options or reconfigure your sofa—your style evolves with SĂCRUM.
